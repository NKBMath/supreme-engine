1. Independent Research

The Superposition Principle and the Riemann Hypothesis

Explores a novel function, G(s), connecting prime distributions and non-trivial zeros of the zeta function. Introduces a wave interference approach to analyze zeta function symmetry, offering insights into fractal-like structures and critical zeros.

Publication: Under review in Ramanujan Journal. Available on Zenodo.

Unified Framework for Total Variation Regularized Optimization

An optimization framework for fluid dynamics and phase transitions using Total Variation (TV) regularization to analyze complex systems like Navier-Stokes and Maxwell equations.

Publication: Under review in Journal of Non-Linear Mathematical Physics. Preprint on ArXiv.

Modular Forms in Combinatorial Optimization

Applies modular forms to combinatorial problems, uncovering modular invariance in the Asymmetric Traveling Salesman Problem.

Publication: Preprint on ArXiv.

Stochastic Optimization Using Ricci Flow

A novel framework for optimizing bounded functions using Fourier series and Ricci flow. Combines sampling methods with inverse Ricci flow to identify global optima in high-dimensional, non-convex problems.

Publication: Preprint on ArXiv.

2. Ph.D. Research

Topological Data Analysis, AI-Driven Predictive Modeling, Mathematical Modeling, and Optimization of Agrivoltaic Systems

Integrates advanced mathematical modeling, AI-driven predictive analytics, and topological data analysis to optimize agrivoltaic systems for sustainable energy and agriculture.

Thermal Analysis of Agrivoltaic Systems (Chapter 6)

Models spatiotemporal energy distributions in agrivoltaic systems, focusing on geometric energy flux and thermal anomalies induced by shading.

Bijection Between Tracking Algorithms and Shadow Distribution (Chapter 5)

Proves a bijection between tracking algorithms and shadow distributions, connecting these dynamics to toroidal symmetry for precise optimization.

Mathematical Framework for Plant Growth Dynamics (Chapter 4)

Introduces a novel framework linking crop models with spatiotemporal radiation patterns, showing non-monotonic relationships in crop yield and shading.

Predicting Corn Yield Using Hybrid AI Models (Chapter 3)

Develops a hybrid LSTM-Transformer model to predict corn yield, integrating shadow geometry with machine learning.

Economic Optimization of Corn Agrivoltaic Farming (Chapter 2)

Identifies optimal economic configurations using iso-profit curves, combining profitability metrics with geometric shadow analysis.
